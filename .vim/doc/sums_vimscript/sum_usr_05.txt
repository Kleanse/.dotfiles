What I learned:

The 'iskeyword' option specifies what characters constitute a word.

String options can use operator+= to append a substring and operator-= to
remove a substring. Options that use delimiters (e.g., a comma ',')
automatically supply or extract them when invoking such operations.


 vim:tw=78:isk=!-~,^*,^\|,^\":ts=8:noet:ft=help:norl:
